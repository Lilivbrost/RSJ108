How to to Win a Dance Battle as a journalist

Assembly time: 30 minutes to forever
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

Are you wondering how to diversify yourself as a journalist? Well, add dancing as your newest skill and become a propper dancer. You never know when you might need to dance battle another journalist for an upcoming story. Follow these steps and become that dancer you were born to be!

  • Things You'll Need!

  • Music
  • A phone
  • Water Bottle
  • Dedication
  • Confidence
  • Your body and mind

  1. Find some great music and create an ultimate playlist on your phone.

    This is necessary to hold the rocket at launch time. Make your best design.

  2. Put on your coolist outfit.

    Remember, that you will be dancing, so don't wear anything that can prohibit movement.

    Wow, that outfit is awesome!

  3. Learn new dance moves.

    A beginner tutorial is recommended for your first time.

  4. Practice, practice, and more practice.

    Dedication is the only way to get better and more confident, just don't forget to stay hydrated.

  5. Challenge your fellow journalist to a dance battle.

    Ask nicely, but don't take it easy on them. If you follow the steps you are sure to win. How does it feel now to be considered a dancer?
